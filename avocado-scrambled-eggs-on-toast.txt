Description:
Scrambled eggs on toast with a layer of avocado dressing.

Serves: 2

Ingredients:
1 avocado
1 teaspoon lemon juice
4 cherry tomatoes
2 eggs
1 tablespoon butter
50ml skimmed milk
Salt and pepper
4 slices of bread

Instructions for making the dressing:
Peal and remove the seed of the avocado
Mash the avocado in a bowl
Add the lemon juice and continue mashing
Mix in the tomatoes and season to taste

Instructions for making the scrambled eggs:
Melt the butter in a pan
Crack the eggs into the pan and immediately beat them whilst adding milk
Add salt and pepperr to taste
Stir continuously until hardened to your preference

Instructions for serving:
Toast the bread
Spread the dressing onto the toast slices
Add scrambled eggs
Serve hot
