Description:
A popular item on my breakfast menu. Omlette on toast.

Ingredients:
1 egg
1 small onion (finely chopped)
1 small mushroom (finely chopped)
1 slice of ham (finely sliced and diced)
1 teaspoon of parsley
2 slices of bread

Instructions:
Fry the onion and mushroom together for 5 minutes
Add the ham and parsley for one minute
In a bowl, beat the mixture with the egg and a teaspoon of water
Fry the mixture till solid, flipping every two minutes to prevent burning
Toast the bread and serve with the omlette
